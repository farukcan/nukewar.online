Nukewar Online

Description: It is a Battleship-style, real-time browser-based online game that features the global world.

Themes: Nuclear warfare, ballistic missiles, nuclear apocalypse, World War 3

Benefits: Increased strategic thinking and time-management capabilities, enhanced political cooperation, imagination of the global context of the world, explanation of the damages that can be caused by nuclear warfare

Parties: Each country is a different party and is controlled by a player. There are 10 (ten) countries. All countries are enemies.

Condition of Victory: Defeat all opponents by bombing their command center cities.

Condition of Defeat: Bombing of the city that command center is located in.

Players and bots: At least 1 player 9 bots. At most 10 players.

Controls: Mouse (Keyboard [For typing messages] )



How to Play

  1. Enter nukewar.online from your browser.

  2. Choose a nickname and click “play”.

  4. The game will start automatically once there are 10 players in the lobby.

  5. By clicking “Do not wait for 10 players”, you can start a game using bots, with the players who do not want to wait.

  7. The game starts in 15 seconds. While waiting, you can read the “How to play?” part.





Controls



Choosing a city

  • You can choose your own or an Enemy city by clicking on its location or from the Country Panel on the top right.

  • Also, you can choose your own cities from the City Panel down below.

Making a move/ Cancelling

  • You can make a move from the Control Panel on the top left after you’ve chosen a city.

  • You can cancel the moves you made from the monitoring panel on the top.



Missile Tracking

  • You can see inbound or outbound missiles from the monitoring panel. By clicking on the Departure/Arrival time, you can focus on the missile.

  • By clicking on a city, you can see the inbound / outbound missiles on the control panel.



Typing a message

  • By clicking on the message box on the chat panel, you can type a message.

  • By clicking Enter, you can start typing a message.



Moves

  • Swap type of cities – move (swap) : 1 minute (Changes the assets in the cities.)

  • Launch Nuclear Missile – Reload : 100 seconds (Missile can be launched to an enemy city which has not been bombed.)

  • Build (Nuclear Launcher or Air Defense): 80 seconds (Must be a civilian city)

  • Repair City: 20 seconds (City must have been bombed)


Types of Cities

Civilian City: Nuclear Launcher or Air Defense can be built in this city.

Command Center: If this city is bombed, the game is lost.

Nuclear Launcher: Bombs can be released from this city to enemy cities.

Air Defense: Protects your cities by shooting inbound enemy missiles.



Rules

  • Played with multiple players or bots. It is a time-based game of strategy.

  • Countries have 5 cities.

  • Countries start with 1 command center and 1 nuclear weapon.

  • If the command center is bombed, the game is lost.

  • All countries are enemies.

  • The country that survives, wins the game.

  • If the nuclear weapon is bombed, it is mandatory to build a new one.

  • A nuclear weapon launched farther away will destroy the target in more time.

  • The command center and nuclear weapon can be transported in every turn. A nuked location cannot be chosen.

  • New nuclear weapon and air defense can be built.


Bot (Artificial intelligence)

It is an artificial intelligence that constantly attacks.

Attack -> Build nuclear launcher if non-existent and available

Attack -> Choose enemy (randomly from those with the least cities)

Attack -> Randomly launch a missile to any enemy city that hasn’t been bombed.



End of the game message:

If this war had taken place in real life, we would have lost the entire world due to radiation and nuclear winter…
